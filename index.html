Emma - artificial intelligence for frontline wildlife crime detection

You can try out the prototype very easily:

  1. Get Telegram messenger, and create an account. It's available for most smartphones and also on desktop.
  2. Send a message to @AskEmmaBot to start a conversation.
  3. Send her either a binomial or part of a common name, and she'll do her best to reply.

Caveats

It's a prototype to demonstrate the concept: the results might be wrong, the search might fail, the service might break. Please report any problems
